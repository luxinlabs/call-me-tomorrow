What to expect

Recall will ask you eight questions — a real conversation, not a form. Speak naturally. There are no wrong answers. Everything you share stays with your future self.

The eight questions

  1. 1 Open doorWhat's going on for you right now?
  2. 2 Peak experienceTell me about a time you felt genuinely like yourself.
  3. 3 The gapWhat's the distance between that and where you are today?
  4. 4 Miracle questionImagine you wake up 5 years from now. What's different?
  5. 5 The obstacleWhat pattern keeps getting in your way?
  6. 6 ValuesWhen you strip everything away — what actually matters most?
  7. 7 The fearWhat would you lose if things actually changed?
  8. 8 ReadinessScale of 1–10, how ready are you to do something different?
